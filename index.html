About Us

The Bigger Than Sports non-profit organization uses sports activities and interactions to provide positive alternatives for at-risk youth in south Seattle. Keeping kids engaged in sports helps steer them away from drugs, violence, and other dangerous behaviors.

Activities

Here are some of the activities we offer:

  • Basketball tournaments
  • Soccer leagues
  • Baseball clinics
  • Community events
